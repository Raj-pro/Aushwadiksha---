Some amazing herb

Butterfly Pea

Clitoria ternatea

Terrain: Riverside, Forest/Wild

Parts Used: Flowers, leaves

Medicine Description: Known for its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate asthma symptoms. The flowers and leaves are often brewed into tea.

Jamun

Syzygium cumini

Terrain: Riverside, Forest/Wild

Parts Used: Fruit, leaves

Medicine Description: Jamun fruit and leaves have been traditionally used for respiratory issues. The fruit can be consumed fresh, and the leaves can be used in teas or decoctions.

Citron

Citrus medica

Terrain: Home Cultivated

Parts Used: Fruit

Medicine Description: Citron fruit is rich in vitamins and has been used in herbal medicine to treat respiratory conditions. It can be consumed as juice or added to herbal preparations.

Malabar Nut

Adenanthera pavonina

Terrain: Forest/Wild

Parts Used: Leaves

Medicine Description: Malabar Nut's leaves are traditionally prepared as a juice with honey and ginger, known for its ability to clear mucus from the respiratory tract.

Five-Leaf Chaste

Vitex negundo

Terrain: Forest/Wild, Home Cultivated

Parts Used: Leaves, roots

Medicine Description: Five-Leaf Chaste has anti-inflammatory properties and is used to treat asthma symptoms. The leaves and roots are typically used in herbal preparations.

Guggal

Commiphora wightii

Terrain: Mountain, Forest/Wild

Parts Used: Gum resin

Medicine Description: Guggal gum resin is known for its anti-inflammatory and bronchodilator properties, which help in managing asthma symptoms.

Alexandrian Laurel

Calophyllum inophyllum

Terrain: Mediterranean forests

Parts Used: Bark

Medicine Description: Alexandrian Laurel bark is used in traditional medicine for its anti-diarrheal properties. It is often prepared as a decoction.

Bael

Aegle marmelos

Terrain: Forests, Riverside

Parts Used: Unripe fruit

Medicine Description: Bael's unripe fruit is known for its ability to manage diarrhoea and dysentery. It is typically dried and used in powdered form.

Curry Leaf

Murraya koenigii

Terrain: Home Cultivated

Parts Used: Leaves

Medicine Description: Curry leaves are known for their digestive properties and can help in treating diarrhoea. They are usually added to food or brewed as tea.

Cutch Tree

Acacia catechu

Terrain: Forests, plains, and cultivated areas

Parts Used: Bark

Medicine Description: Cutch Tree bark has astringent properties that can help in treating diarrhoea. It is typically used in the form of a decoction.

Mahuva

Madhuca longifolia

Terrain: Plains

Parts Used: Bark

Medicine Description: Mahuva bark is used traditionally for its antidiarrheal effects. It is often prepared as a decoction.

Vetiver

Chrysopogon zizanioides

Terrain: Plains, Riverside

Parts Used: Roots

Medicine Description: Vetiver roots are known for their cooling and digestive properties, often used in traditional remedies for diarrhoea.
